MISSION & VISION

Our MISSION

Our Mission is to REACH the less privileged , we can't reach them and not FEED, thereafter, we TEACH them the skills required to empower them for a brighter future.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to remove the financial barrier between African youths and a bright future by equipping them with the skills necessary to become financially independent. Africa has been blessed with some of the brightest minds that have ever walked the earth. Those minds had a gift. The gift of education is an opportunity to learn. We envision a continent where every African youth has access to a good education. We believe achieving this will secure the future of the next generation. For every African youth whose access to education is being denied by the gate of financial inadequacy, we want to help. Knowledge is power.
